About BFB

Bicycle Friendly business(BFB) is an initiative by the Council for Active Mobility(CFAM) to create safe bicycle infrastructure in Bengaluru with the support of local businesses.

We encourage businesses to welcome and prioritise cyclists. Our mission is to make our city congestion free, safer, calmer, and more liveable.

For businesses

If you'd like to participate in the program and help in our mission, please reach out to us and we shall get back to you at the earliest.

For individuals

We are a volunteer group of active cyclists. We're always looking for motivated individuals to join our cause. If you'd like to volunteer, the best way is to join the conversation on our Discord server.
