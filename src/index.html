Bicycle Friendly Business

About BFB

Bicycle Friendly Business (BFB) is an initiative by Council for Active Mobility(CFAM) to enable businesses to participate in making the city liveable and climate resilient by encouraging active travel modes like cycling.

For businesses

If you would like to be associated as a green business that support active travel, we will list you on our site/promote you on social media. In return you commit to welcoming customers on active travel modes like cycling, provide them with facilities like safe parking and other discounts.

If interested, please reach out to us and we shall get back to you at the earliest.

For individuals

We are a volunteer group of enthusiasts who run various campaigns to make the city more active travel friendly. We're always looking for motivated individuals to join our cause. If you'd like to volunteer, the best way is to join the conversation on our Discord server.
